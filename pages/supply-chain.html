Capabilities

Beyond freight — strategic SCM

For shippers ready to treat their supply chain as a competitive advantage, not a cost centre.

Multimodal Route Planning

We model your supply chain end-to-end and identify the optimal mode split — air for urgent, sea for bulk, road for last leg — that balances cost, speed, and risk.

  • Network optimization modelling
  • Cost-speed trade-off analysis
  • Risk-adjusted routing

Vendor & Supplier Management

One coordinator manages the entire vendor base — POs, inbound bookings, document collection, and quality checks. Your team focuses on the product.

  • Supplier onboarding
  • PO & document control
  • Vendor SLA reporting

KPI Dashboards & Analytics

Live dashboards showing on-time performance, cost per kg, lane utilization, and exception rates. The metrics that prove value, not just activity.

  • Custom KPI dashboards
  • Cost per kg / TEU tracking
  • On-time delivery reporting

Quarterly Business Reviews

Every quarter we sit down with your team — review the data, surface opportunities, agree on the next quarter's targets. Continuous improvement, formalised.

  • QBR reports & presentations
  • Cost savings opportunities
  • Network & mode redesign
