Capabilities

When standard freight won't fit

Project cargo is its own discipline. Different equipment, different paperwork, different mindset. Here's what we bring.

Out-of-Gauge & Breakbulk

Cargo that doesn't fit in a container — ROCONs, flat-racks, breakbulk vessels, MAFI trailers. We've moved everything from yachts to refinery columns.

  • Flat-rack & open-top FCL
  • Breakbulk & MPV chartering
  • ROCONs & MAFI trailers

Heavy Lift & Crane Operations

Engineered lift plans for cargo above 50 tonnes. Our riggers work with port-side gantries, mobile cranes, and offshore heavy-lift vessels.

  • Engineered lift plans & CAD
  • Mobile crane coordination
  • Offshore heavy-lift vessels

Route Surveys & Permits

Before the cargo moves, we move first. Physical route surveys check bridge clearances, road widths, and obstacles. Permits filed in every transit country.

  • Physical route surveys
  • Bridge & tunnel clearances
  • Multi-country permits

Escorts & Specialized Transport

Multi-axle hydraulic trailers, beam-trucks, and SPMTs. Police escorts where required, route closures where mandated. One coordinator manages the whole convoy.

  • Self-propelled modular trailers
  • Beam-truck & girder bridges
  • Police & civil escorts
Project Workflow

Engineered end-to-end

Project cargo isn't a booking — it's a programme. Our four-phase approach keeps everything on schedule.

01

Feasibility

Cargo dimensions, weights, and routes audited. Bottlenecks flagged before the contract is signed.

02

Engineering

Lifting plans, route surveys, transport drawings, and method statements — produced in-house, signed by chartered engineers.

03

Execution

Equipment mobilized, permits secured, police escorts coordinated. Daily status reports from the convoy.

04

Handover

Cargo positioned at site, signed acceptance, final documentation, post-project debrief.
