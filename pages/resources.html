Frequently Asked

The questions we hear most

Straight answers — written from years of fielding these exact questions over the phone.

How quickly can I get a quote?

Standard quotes within 4 working hours during Dubai office hours (Sun–Thu, 9:00–18:00 GST). Urgent or complex shipments — project cargo, charter, dangerous goods — within 24 hours with full feasibility notes.

Do you handle customs clearance in destination countries?

Yes — we hold customs broker licenses or work with licensed in-country partners across the regions we serve. That includes the UAE, Iraq, Turkey, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Pakistan, India, Azerbaijan, Georgia, all major EU members, the UK, and China.

Can you ship into Afghanistan?

Yes. Afghanistan is one of our specialty corridors. We operate air freight via Dubai (DXB) and Istanbul (IST) into Kabul (KBL) and Herat (HEA), plus overland trucking via Pakistan (Torkham) and Uzbekistan (Hairatan). Customs clearance handled by our agents on the ground.

What's the minimum shipment size you'll accept?

There's no hard minimum. We've shipped single-pallet LCL ocean and 5kg air parcels alongside full container loads. For small shipments, we recommend our LCL consolidation services — competitive rates with weekly sailings.

Do you handle hazardous and dangerous goods?

Yes — we're IATA-certified for air DG and IMDG-trained for ocean. We handle Class 1 (explosives) through Class 9 (miscellaneous), subject to carrier capacity and destination regulations. Tell us the UN number and we'll confirm feasibility.

How does cargo insurance work?

All shipments come with limited carrier liability (which is rarely enough). We offer optional All-Risk marine/air cargo insurance — typically 0.15–0.4% of CIF value. We can issue a certificate within the same day as booking.

Can I track my shipment in real time?

Yes. Every booking gets a tracking link with milestone updates — pickup, gate-in, vessel/flight departure, arrival, customs clearance, and delivery. Most milestones include proactive email/SMS alerts.

What payment terms do you offer?

Prepaid for first-time shipments. Credit terms (NET 15 or NET 30) available for established accounts subject to a short credit review. We accept wire transfer, credit card (with surcharge), and approved corporate accounts.
