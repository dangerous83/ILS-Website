Warehousing Capabilities

Storage that doesn't stay still

Warehouses are nothing without the operations behind them. Our facilities are run by people who understand that every minute on the rack costs you money.

Bonded & Free-Zone Storage

Duty-deferred storage in Dubai's free zones and bonded warehouses. Ideal for re-export, regional distribution, and inventory that may move on without entering the local market.

  • JAFZA & DAFZA free-zone storage
  • Customs-bonded warehousing
  • Re-export & transit cargo
  • Long-term & flexible-term contracts

Cross-Docking

Inbound containers split, re-palletised, and dispatched on outbound trucks within hours — no storage cost, minimal handling. Built for high-velocity SKUs.

  • Container devanning
  • Re-palletisation & re-labelling
  • Same-day outbound dispatch
  • FCL → LTL conversion

Pick, Pack & Kitting

Order-fulfilment-grade pick and pack for e-commerce, retail replenishment, and B2B distribution. Kitting, bundling, and value-added services in-house.

  • Each-pick & case-pick fulfilment
  • Bundling & kitting
  • Custom packaging & labelling
  • Returns processing

Cloud Inventory Visibility

Real-time stock visibility through our WMS — accessible from the web or pluggable into your ERP via API. No more guessing what's on the floor.

  • Live WMS dashboard
  • API integration with your ERP
  • Cycle counts & full inventories
  • Stock-aging & SKU velocity reports
Warehousing Process

From inbound dock to outbound truck

A clear sequence — every SKU accounted for, every movement logged, every cost transparent.

01

Onboarding

SKU master setup, volume & storage requirements, integration with your ERP. Live in days, not months.

02

Receive & Putaway

Inbound goods receipted, inspected, scanned, and putaway against the WMS — visible in your dashboard the same hour.

03

Pick, Pack & Ship

Orders picked, packed, and dispatched against your SLAs — air, sea, road, or last-mile courier.

04

Reconcile & Report

Weekly KPI reports, monthly stock reconciliations, quarterly business reviews. Total visibility, total accountability.
