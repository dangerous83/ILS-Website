Sectors We Serve

Specialists for every cargo

Eight industry verticals with dedicated account teams, compliance protocols, and handling certifications. Click any sector to talk to a specialist.

Healthcare & Pharma

GDP-certified cold-chain logistics for vaccines, biologics, and medical devices. Temperature-controlled +2°C to +8°C, dry ice for −80°C, full chain-of-custody documentation.

  • GDP-compliant air & ocean
  • Active & passive reefer containers
  • Temperature data loggers

Retail & E-commerce

Fulfillment, last-mile, and reverse logistics built for D2C and omnichannel retail. Scale up for peak season without breaking your margins.

  • Direct-to-consumer fulfillment
  • Peak season capacity guarantees
  • Returns & reverse logistics

Manufacturing & Automotive

Just-in-time inbound logistics, plant-to-plant transfers, and aftermarket parts distribution for global manufacturing operations. Tier-1 supplier-ready.

  • JIT & JIS inbound delivery
  • Tier-1 supplier integration
  • Aftermarket parts distribution

Oil, Gas & Energy

Project cargo, heavy-lift, and hazardous materials for upstream, midstream, and renewable energy. From refinery modules to wind-turbine blades.

  • Out-of-gauge & heavy-lift
  • IMO-class DG handling
  • Route surveys & permits

Technology & Electronics

Secure, high-value cargo handling with ESD protection and serial-number-level visibility for the world's tech supply chains.

  • ESD-safe packaging & handling
  • Serial-level chain of custody
  • High-value cargo insurance

Food & Perishables

Reefer logistics, cold-chain visibility, and HACCP-compliant handling for fresh produce, seafood, dairy, and frozen goods worldwide.

  • Reefer FCL & LCL ocean
  • HACCP-compliant warehousing
  • Temperature-logged transit

Humanitarian & NGO

Emergency relief shipments to complex destinations — Afghanistan, Yemen, Sudan, and beyond. Field-tested customs expertise where it counts most.

  • Charter & emergency air
  • Donor-compliant documentation
  • Conflict-zone routing

Luxury & Fashion

White-glove handling for jewellery, watches, fashion, and fine art. Climate-controlled, bonded warehousing, and discreet last-mile.

  • Climate-controlled handling
  • Bonded high-value storage
  • White-glove last-mile
