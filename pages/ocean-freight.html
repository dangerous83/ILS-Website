Ocean Freight Capabilities

Every container, every lane

From a single pallet of LCL cargo to full reefer programmes, we handle the full ocean spectrum with direct carrier contracts and operational expertise on both ends.

FCL — Full Container Load

20', 40', 40HC, and reefer containers on every major lane. Direct rates with Maersk, MSC, CMA-CGM, Hapag-Lloyd, ONE, and regional carriers.

  • 20', 40', 40HC dry containers
  • Reefer (40HC, integrated)
  • High-cube & open-top
  • Direct master/house BLs

LCL — Less than Container Load

Weekly LCL consolidations from major hubs into the Middle East, Central Asia, South Asia, and beyond. Tight rates from a single pallet upwards.

  • Weekly sailings worldwide
  • Consolidation at origin CFS
  • Deconsolidation at destination
  • From 1 CBM upwards

Out-of-Gauge & Specialty

Flat-rack, open-top, and breakbulk for oversized cargo. Surveyed lashing plans, port-handling permits, and stevedoring coordinated in-house.

  • Flat-rack & open-top
  • Breakbulk & RoRo
  • Lashing & securing plans
  • Heavy-lift port supervision

Door-to-Door

Full origin pickup, export clearance, ocean leg, import clearance, and final mile — under one contract and one waybill.

  • Pre-carriage trucking
  • Origin & destination customs
  • Inland on-carriage
  • Single accountable contract
Ocean Freight Process

Booked, sailed, delivered

Predictable, transparent, and tracked at every stage — from origin gate-in to destination delivery.

01

Quote & Routing

We compare direct rates across carriers, recommend the routing, and lock in a sailing date.

02

Pickup & Gate-In

Container delivered, stuffed, and gated into the origin terminal before the carrier's cut-off.

03

On the Water

Live vessel tracking, transshipment alerts, and proactive ETA updates if anything changes.

04

Cleared & Delivered

Destination customs pre-filed, container picked up at the port, delivered to your door.
