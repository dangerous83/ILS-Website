Cookie Policy

What cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

Last updated: 19 May 2026

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your device so that it can recognise you on your next visit, remember your preferences, or measure how the site is used. Cookies are an industry standard and most websites use them.

2. Cookies we use

We keep our use of cookies deliberately minimal. The cookies on ilsmtc.com fall into the following categories:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the site to function — page navigation, form submission, security. Session
Preference Remembers basic UI preferences such as language or display state. Up to 1 year
Analytics Anonymous measurement of how visitors use the site — page views, time on page, traffic sources. We use this only to improve the site. Up to 2 years

3. Third-party cookies

Some pages embed services from third parties (for example, social media icons or fonts). These third parties may set their own cookies, which we do not control. Where applicable, you can review and adjust your preferences directly through the relevant third-party service.

4. Managing cookies

You can accept, refuse, or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

Please note that if you block essential cookies, parts of the website may not work as expected.

6. Do Not Track

Where your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal, we respect it on our own analytics. Third-party services embedded on the site may handle the signal differently.

7. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL and the "Last updated" date will reflect any change.

8. Contact
