Our Solutions

Logistics solutions, end-to-end

Six integrated solutions that bundle our air, sea, and road services into outcomes — moving your cargo from origin to destination with one accountable partner.

Freight Forwarding

Air, sea, and road handled by one team under one booking. We coordinate every leg, every carrier, and every milestone from pickup to final delivery.

Project Cargo & Heavy Lift

Out-of-gauge, oversized, and breakbulk cargo handled end-to-end. Route surveys, permits, escort vehicles, and specialized rigging — engineered in-house.

Customs & Trade Compliance

Licensed brokers, HS-code classification, FTA advisory, and AEO-grade documentation. We clear your cargo so it never sits at the border.

Warehousing & Distribution

Bonded and free-zone storage at strategic hubs. Cross-docking, pick-and-pack, kitting, and onward distribution — with cloud-based inventory visibility.

Supply Chain Management

Multimodal route planning, vendor coordination, KPI dashboards, and quarterly business reviews. One partner for the whole supply chain.

Time-Critical & Charter

Same-day air, urgent road, and full-aircraft charters when standard transit isn't fast enough. Spare parts, AOG, humanitarian — we move it now.

Wherever the Road Goes

If it can be reached, we'll get it there

Mountain passes, desert corridors, remote border posts, and roads that don't show up on most maps — the hard miles are where ILS does its best work. When a lane looks impossible, we plan the route, clear the crossing, and keep your cargo moving.

  • High-altitude & rough-terrain routing across the CIS, Central Asia, and the SILKWAY corridor.
  • Cross-border & landlocked delivery with TIR-Carnet trucks, pre-cleared customs, and escort coordination.
  • Drivers who know every post — local knowledge on the ground and 24/7 dispatch behind the wheel.
