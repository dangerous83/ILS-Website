Air Freight Capabilities

Every shipment, every speed

Whether you need a single piece on the next flight out or a weekly consolidation, our air desk has the right product for the job.

Express & Time-Critical

Same-day, next-flight-out, and AOG shipments. Hand-carry, on-board courier, and door-to-door with a guaranteed wheels-up time.

  • Next-Flight-Out (NFO)
  • Hand-carry & on-board courier
  • Aircraft-on-Ground (AOG) parts
  • Same-day pickup & clearance

Dangerous Goods (IATA)

IATA-certified DG handling from Class 1 to Class 9. Full DGR documentation, packing-group verification, and licensed shippers' declarations.

  • UN-classified DG shipments
  • Lithium battery handling
  • DGR-compliant labelling
  • In-house DG safety adviser

Temperature-Controlled

Pharma, perishable, and life-sciences cargo with active and passive temperature integrity — from origin packing to delivery.

  • Active & passive cold-chain
  • CEIV Pharma routes
  • Reefer ULDs & thermal blankets
  • Continuous temperature monitoring

Charter & Full Aircraft

Full or part-charter on freighter and passenger aircraft when scheduled capacity isn't enough. Worldwide aircraft sourcing in hours.

  • Part-charter buyout

  • Outsized cargo on AN-124 / B747F
Air Freight Process

Wheels up in hours, not days

Four steps from inquiry to airport — no handoffs, one accountable contact.

01

Quote in Hours

Send dimensions, weight, origin, destination. We confirm rates and the next available flight slot the same business day.

02

Pickup & Pack

We collect, prepare the AWB, file pre-alerts, and handle export customs — your cargo lands at the airport gate-ready.

03

Track in Real Time

Live flight tracking, milestone alerts at uplift, transit, and arrival. One inbox, all updates.

04

Cleared & Delivered

Destination clearance pre-filed, last-mile dispatched on landing. Proof of delivery and invoice within 24 hours.
