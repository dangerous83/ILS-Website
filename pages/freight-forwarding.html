What's Included

One partner, every leg

Multimodal freight forwarding bundles everything you need to move cargo internationally — under one team, one platform, one invoice.

Air Freight

Express, standard, and consolidations from major hubs into 200+ destinations. Direct AWB issuance via IATA accreditation.

  • Same-day & next-flight-out
  • Dangerous goods (IATA)
  • Temperature-controlled cargo
  • Charter & on-board courier

Ocean Freight

FCL, LCL, reefer, and out-of-gauge across every major trade lane. Weekly sailings, direct rates, and full BL handling.

  • FCL (20', 40', 40HC, reefer)
  • LCL consolidations
  • Out-of-gauge & flat-rack
  • Door-to-door or port-to-port

Road & Cross-Border

FTL and LTL across the GCC, EU, Central Asia, and the SILKWAY corridor. TIR-Carnet trucking with on-board GPS and 24/7 dispatch.

  • Full & Less than truckload
  • Reefer & ADR-certified
  • TIR-Carnet international
  • Cross-border customs transit

Customs & Documentation

Licensed brokers, HS classification, FTA filings, and pre-clearance handled in-house. Your cargo never sits at the border.

  • Import & export clearance
  • HS classification
  • FTA & AEO documentation
  • Bonded transit handling
How It Works

From inquiry to delivery

A four-step routine — every shipment, every mode, every time.

01

Inquiry

Tell us origin, destination, cargo, and Incoterms. We respond with a multimodal quote in hours.

02

Booking

You approve. We book carriers, schedule pickup, and pre-file customs documentation.

03

In Transit

Live tracking, milestone alerts, and a single point of contact through every leg.

04

Delivered

Cleared, delivered, signed-off. Proof of delivery and final invoice — same day.
