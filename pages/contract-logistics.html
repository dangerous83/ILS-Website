Contract Logistics Capabilities

One partner, your whole operation

Contract logistics is more than renting space. It's an outsourced operation — people, processes, and technology — that we design around your business and run under one SLA, so warehousing, distribution, and inventory stop being four vendors and become one accountable team.

Dedicated & Shared Facilities

Dedicated or multi-user warehouse space sized to your volume — scale up for peak and down again without carrying the capex or the property risk yourself.

  • Dedicated & multi-user warehousing
  • Free-zone & customs-bonded options
  • Rapid scale-up for peak seasons
  • Asset-light, opex-based model

Integrated Transport & Distribution

Inbound freight, line-haul, and last-mile delivery bundled with storage under one roof — one desk, one bill, one point of accountability for the whole flow.

  • GCC & cross-border distribution
  • Last-mile & B2B delivery
  • Air, sea & road handoffs managed
  • Carrier-neutral, best-rate routing

Inventory & Order Management

A cloud WMS that plugs into your ERP or store, giving you real-time stock, order orchestration, and the audit trail your finance team asks for.

  • Cloud WMS with API / EDI integration
  • Real-time stock & order status
  • Demand & replenishment support
  • Cycle counts, audits & SKU reporting

Value-Added Services

The shop-floor work that turns a pallet into a retail-ready order — done in-house, so your product ships branded, compliant, and complete.

  • Kitting, bundling & light assembly
  • Labelling, re-packing & barcoding
  • Quality control & inspection
  • Returns & reverse logistics
How We Onboard

From blueprint to steady state

A structured transition — modelled before it's committed, phased before it goes live, and measured from day one.

01

Design & Model

We map your flows, volumes, and service levels, then model the footprint, headcount, and cost per unit — so you sign off on numbers, not promises.

02

Implement & Integrate

Facility fit-out, WMS-to-ERP integration, and team training run as a phased go-live — with a fallback plan at every milestone.

03

Operate & Fulfil

Daily receiving, storage, picking, and distribution run to agreed KPIs by a dedicated team that knows your product as well as you do.

04

Measure & Optimise

Live KPI dashboards, quarterly business reviews, and continuous-improvement targets written into the contract itself.
