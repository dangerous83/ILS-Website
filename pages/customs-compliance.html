Clearance Workflow

Pre-cleared before it lands

By the time your cargo arrives, the paperwork is filed, the duties are calculated, and the cargo is queued for release.

01

Document Pre-check

Commercial invoice, packing list, COO, and any permits reviewed before cargo ships. Errors caught early.

02

HS & Duty Calc

Classifications confirmed, duty estimates issued, FTA opportunities surfaced. No surprise at clearance.

03

Pre-filing

Customs declarations submitted electronically before cargo arrives. Many ports release on arrival.

04

Release & Records

Cargo cleared, duties paid, archive copies filed for your audit trail. Standard retention: 5 years.
