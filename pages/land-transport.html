Land Transport Capabilities

Every load, every border

From a single pallet on an LTL line-haul to a full TIR-Carnet truck running Istanbul to Tashkent — we run the road leg in-house with vetted carriers.

FTL & LTL

Full-truckload and less-than-truckload across the GCC, Europe, and CIS. Dedicated trailers or shared linehauls, depending on volume and cost target.

  • FTL — dedicated tractor & trailer
  • LTL — shared groupage
  • Curtainsider, box, & tilt
  • Daily & scheduled departures

Cross-Border & TIR

TIR-Carnet trucking across CMR/TIR jurisdictions. Pre-cleared customs, escort coordination, and route surveys before the first wheel turns.

  • TIR-Carnet international
  • CMR documentation
  • Border-post pre-clearance
  • Escort & permit handling

Reefer & Specialised

Temperature-controlled trailers for pharma, perishables, and frozen cargo. ADR-certified vehicles and drivers for dangerous goods.

  • Mono- & multi-temperature reefers
  • ADR Class 1 to 9 capability
  • Tank trucks for liquids
  • Continuous temperature logging

24/7 Dispatch & Tracking

GPS-tracked fleet with a live dispatch desk that doesn't sleep. Live ETAs, geo-fence alerts, and a direct line to the driver when it matters.

  • Live GPS on every shipment
  • Geo-fenced milestone alerts
  • 24/7 Dubai dispatch desk
  • Customer-portal tracking link
Land Transport Process

From inquiry to unloading bay

Predictable border crossings, predictable transit times, predictable rates — that's the job.

01

Route & Quote

We map the route, calculate transit (including border wait times), and lock in a fixed rate.

02

Pickup

Trailer arrives at your dock on the agreed slot. Loading supervised by our team where required.

03

Linehaul & Borders

Driver dispatched, GPS live, customs pre-filed at each border post. Alerts at every milestone.

04

Delivered

Signed POD returned the same day. Reconciliation and invoice within 24 hours.
