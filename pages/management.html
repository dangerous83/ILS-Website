Our Team

Highly Experienced Logistics Team

ILS is run by people who came up through operations — customs brokers, ocean and air specialists, route planners, and on-the-ground coordinators spread across our five hubs. They pair two decades of classic forwarding know-how with modern technology, AI-supported tracking, and real-time digital visibility, so every shipment is handled by someone who knows the lane and backed by tools that keep you informed.

From a relief shipment into Kabul to a block-train along the SILKWAY corridor or a time-critical charter out of Dubai, our specialists stay personally involved in the moves that matter — deep know-how, fast decisions, and a single point of accountability from first quote to final delivery.
